Why the Hybrid Works

From "The Legal Futurist"

We keep the Deep Midnight Blue base. This anchors the "Legal Certainty" aspect. It feels grounded, authoritative, and expensive (like a high-end law firm or bank).

From "The Creative Architect"

We inject Indigo/Violet as the primary action color. This brings the "Creative Spark" and "AI Magic" feel. It prevents the app from feeling too cold or corporate.

The "Teal" Kicker

We add Electric Teal as a highlight color (icons, success states, AI moments). This signals "Innovation" and "Freshness," bridging the gap between the dark authority and the creative purple.
